## Sensor Board Interconnect Strategy

**Date:** 2026-04-06

- Short internal cable (~40mm) inside single enclosure considered acceptable for AS5600/hall daughterboard
- Keeps main board compact and avoids extending PCB length by ~40mm
- Requires defined padout and strain relief strategy
- 6 pin layout (2 power, 1 ground, 2 I2c, and one switch)
- considering 1.25mm pitch ribbon connect
